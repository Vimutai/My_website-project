OMI Wellness Program

At OMI, we help people feel whole again by creating moments of stillness, joy, and connection that restore clarity, resilience, and a sense of purpose. Our mission is to support individuals navigating high-pressure environments by offering one-day, peaceful outdoor experiences designed to nurture calm, reflection, play, and self-awareness in serene, alcohol-free settings.
